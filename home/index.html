Biography

Brenden Davis is a multidisciplinary artist from Jackson, Mississippi whose work spans painting, animation, and digital design. Blending surrealism, abstraction, and Black interiority, their practice builds dreamlike figures, interfaces, and landscapes that explore memory, technology, and the shifting boundaries of self.
