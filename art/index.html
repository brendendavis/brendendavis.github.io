Fine Art

Brenden Davis is a visual artist based in Jackson, Mississippi working across painting, mixed media, and sculptural installation. His work explores the interior landscapes of Black life in relation to technology, infrastructure, and systems of power. He is currently a 2026 Artist-in-Residence at Civil Rights Corps.
