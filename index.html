Biography

Brenden Davis is a multidisciplinary artist
from Jackson, Mississippi,
working in painting, graphic design,
and animation.
Their art blends surrealism and abstraction with explorations of the Black
experience, crafting dreamlike figures and landscapes that challenge
societal perceptions and spark dialogue.
